About Us

With a 30-year history, Shri Ganesh Pyro Tech is a well-known producer of premium firecrackers. Our credo is that every seasonal celebration should be joyous and secure. We use our wide variety of firecrackers for this. With over 300 different types of crackers produced and sold each year, we are among the most sought-after products in the Sivakasi area and across the nation. We have been a wholesale and retail trader of firecracker supplies for the last 15 years. We take great care to guarantee that all of our orders are delivered promptly and affordably, and our products are renowned for their safety.

Values

Do's

Instructions
Display fireworks as per the instructions mentioned on the pack.
Outdoor
Use fireworks only outdoor
Branded Fireworks
Buy fireworks from authorized / reputed manufacturers only.
Distance
Light only one firework at a time, by one person. Others should watch from a safe distance.
Water
Keep two buckets of water handy. In the event of fire or any mishap.

Don'ts

Don't make tricks
Never make your own fireworks.
Don't relight
Never try to re-light or pick up fireworks that have not ignited fully.
Don't carry it
Never carry fireworks in your pockets
Don't Touch it
After fireworks display never pick up fireworks that may be left over, they still may be active.
Don't wear loose clothes
Do not wear loose clothing while using fireworks.
