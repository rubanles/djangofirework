About Us

With a 30-year history, Shri Ganesh Pyro Tech is a well-known producer of premium firecrackers. Our credo is that every seasonal celebration should be joyous and secure. We use our wide variety of firecrackers for this. With over 300 different types of crackers produced and sold each year, we are among the most sought-after products in the Sivakasi area and across the nation. We have been a wholesale and retail trader of firecracker supplies for the last 15 years. We take great care to guarantee that all of our orders are delivered promptly and affordably, and our products are renowned for their safety.
We are the leading supplier of Sparklers, Ground Chakkars, Flower Pots, Fountains, Fancy Crackers, Sound Crackers, Novelty Fireworks, Rockets, Bombs, Twinkling Stars, Elite Crackers, Fancy Deluxe Fountains, Loose Crackers, Electric Crackers, Super Blast Wala Crackers, Fancy Novelties, Multi Color Shots, Aerial Color Novelties, Comets and Fireworks Gift Box.


Vision and Mission

To be the best wholesaler of all kinds of fancy crackers and gift boxes, as well as the dominant player in innovative and colorful crackers. Our purpose is to provide our valued customers with high-quality, innovative fireworks at a reasonable price, illuminating all of their celebrations.
